CYPRESS Website

This was my final project for my "Introduction to Software Engineering" course in 2021. It was a 3 person group project.

The site is meant to mimic many attributes found on the Toronto City website such as Login, Register, Change Information, Report a Problem, Frequently Asked Questions and more.

I was responsible for creating and designing the website, whereas my colleagues worked on the back-end portion of the website.

The completed project consists of over a dozen webpages as well as a built in Google Maps API found on the "Report a Problem" page.

We ended up getting a final grade of 100% on the project because we met all of the requirements needed for the website.
